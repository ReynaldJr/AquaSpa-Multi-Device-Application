Facial Cleanser

Indulge your skin in the ultimate pampering experience with AquaSpa Facial Cleanser. Formulated with a delicate blend of nourishing botanical extracts and gentle cleansing agents, our facial cleanser is designed to effectively remove impurities, excess oil, and makeup, while maintaining the skin's natural moisture balance. The luxurious creamy texture glides effortlessly across the skin, leaving it feeling refreshed, revitalized, and deeply cleansed.

Enriched with antioxidants and vitamins, our facial cleanser helps to protect the skin against environmental stressors and promote a more radiant complexion. The soothing properties of chamomile and aloe vera extract calm and soothe the skin, while Hyaluronic Acid replenishes moisture, leaving the skin soft, supple, and hydrated. Suitable for all skin types, our facial cleanser provides a gentle yet thorough cleanse, unveiling a clean and luminous complexion.
